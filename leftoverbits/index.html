algorithmic composition with a modified pythagorean tuning

Leftover Bits is an algorithmically composed album using SuperCollider and PureData using a variation of Pythagorean tuning. Instead of generating musical intervals only by multiplying by powers of 2 and 3, I included powers of 5 as well.
