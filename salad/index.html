physics models and chladni figures

Salad is a javascript physics engine and resonance visualization made by Noura Howell, Lucas Culler and myself, inspired by Chladni Figures.

We perform the discrete wave equation on a grid of pixels to propogate waves from a source. Every pixel on the canvas has a current position (how far into/out of the screen it is) and a current velocity. At every time 'tick', we update every pixel's velocity using the surrounding 4 pixels on each side. If the average positions of the surrounding pixels are higher than a given pixel, we add a little bit to that pixel's velocity. If the average of the surrounding pixel positions are lower we subtract from the velocity instead. We also apply a small amount of velocity damping.

The project is still in the beginning stages but you can check out the full project or view the source code.
