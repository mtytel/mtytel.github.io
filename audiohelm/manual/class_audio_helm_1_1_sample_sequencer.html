Sample Sequencer

Switch to Scripting

A sequencer of notes over time that will send its note on/off events to a Sampler instance that is attatched to the same object.

Sequencer Usage

  • Adding Notes - Click on the cell in the sequencer you would like to add it to. Click and drag left and right to create a repeating sequence of the same note.
  • Deleting Notes - Click on an existing note. Click on a note and drag left and right to delete a section of notes.
  • Resizing Notes - Click on the left or right edge to resize a note in that direction.
  • Changing Velocity - Velocity intensities are shown at the bottom of the sequencer. Click and drag up and down to change the velocity of the note above. The color of the note will change, the more saturated the higher the velocity.
  • Test a Note - Click on the piano section on the left side of the sequencer to try out a note.

◆ Clear Sequencer

Removes all notes from the sequencer.

◆ Length

The length of the sequence measured in sixteenth notes.

◆ Division

The division of the graphical sequencer. Changing this does not change the sequence of notes, but can makes it easier to add notes in different rhythms.
